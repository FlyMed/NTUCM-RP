Dean's Message

Founded as the first medical college in 1897, National Taiwan University College of Medicine (NTUCM) is the leading medical college in Taiwan and in Asia Pacific, with a centennial history of excellence in medical education and research.

We recruit the best students in the country every year, and our mission is to educate and train the best medical personnel to serve people and meet our society’s health needs. Most importantly, NTUCM has cultivated many outstanding alumni and faculty who are the leaders and role models in medical education and biomedical research in Taiwan.

Over the last centennial period, NTUCM and the affiliated National Taiwan University Hospital (NTUH) has adhered to the outstanding tradition, and been devoted to develop into a world class medical center in Asia. We aim to nurture eminent and elite medical professionals, and to promote innovative biomedical research. With the effort to accomplish the missions, we have forever committed unreservedly to teaching and biomedical research.

To promote multidisciplinary research and translational medicine in the post genomic era, we have established the Center for Genomic Medicine with state-of-the-art core facilities which can provide technology platform for faculty to perform cutting-edge biomedical research.

We wish to introduce our faculty’s outstanding research achievements with this Research Portal, and we welcome the opportunities to extend the international collaboration.
